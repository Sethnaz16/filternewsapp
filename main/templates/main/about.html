Why we created Check Your Source

In this day and age, "fake news" is everywhere. When you can't be sure whether a news source is telling the truth or seeking political or monetary gain, the average citizen is hurt the most.


We need ways to help verify the accuracy of our news that are easily accessible and clearly understood. We have developed just such a tool so that you too can determine whether you're being fed facts, or fiction.
